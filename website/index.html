🔁 Precision A-B Loop

Trap the exact phrase. Repeat it until it sticks.

Set point A and point B down to the millisecond, then let Pawcast loop the passage hands-free. Built for shadowers, transcribers, and musicians drilling a lick.

  • ✓Millisecond A/B markers — set from the current time, drag on the waveform, or jump to either edge.
  • ✓Nudge, halve & double the loop window without losing your place.
  • ✓Loop count & gap — repeat N times with a silent pause between reps, or loop infinitely.
  • ✓BPM quantization for musicians — snap loop length to musical beats.
  • ✓Shareable loops — export loop points & settings as a link.

🎙️ Shadowing Recorder

Speak along, record every take, compare to the original.

Shadowing mode arms your mic to the loop. Record as many takes as you like, play them back against the source, and dial in the mix between the two tracks.

  • ✓Unlimited takes per passage, saved automatically to your device.
  • ✓Per-sentence recording — auto-arm on each sentence for structured drills.
  • ✓Independent track volume for original vs. your shadow track.
  • ✓Stop delay so the recorder captures your full trailing syllable.

📝 Smart Transcripts + AI

Read along. Tap a word to jump. Select a phrase to understand it.

Generate a word-timed transcript with Whisper, Gemini, or Groq — or upload your own. Every word is a seek point. Highlight any text and ask the AI to explain or translate it in context.

  • ✓Word-level timestamps & speaker diarization — click a word to seek there.
  • ✓Inline translations under each line in your target language.
  • ✓Select → Explain — grammar, nuance, and vocabulary on demand, streamed live.
  • ✓Save to Glossary to review the phrase later with its original audio.
